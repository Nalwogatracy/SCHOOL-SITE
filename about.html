St Bensdorf is a standard high school located in Gayaza-zirobwe road. It is an adventist founded school formed in 1997.We have been in the industry for 26 years and our perfomance is ground-breaking.We care about people's talents and our aim is to raise our students' talents,social and spiritual lives.

Our school is an outstanding school in Gayaza with qualified teachers and staff which is ready to handle our students with care.

Bensdoff is accredited by the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB). The school offers a variety of subjects, including English, Mathematics, Science, History, and Art. Bensdoff has a strong extracurricular program, with activities such as sports, music, and drama. The school has a beautiful campus with a variety of facilities, including a library, science labs, and a computer lab. Bensdoff is a great option for students who are looking for a challenging and rewarding education. The school's strong academic record and wide range of programs make it a popular choice for students from all over Uganda.

Detailed Us

Bensdoff Senior Secondary School is a private, co-educational day school located in Gayaza, a suburb of Kampala, Uganda. The school was founded in 1998 and offers a wide range of academic and extracurricular programs for students in grades 8 to 13. Bensdoff is a well-respected school with a strong academic record.
